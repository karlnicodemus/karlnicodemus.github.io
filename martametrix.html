Marta Metrix

This is a web application that connects Marta traffic to poverty areas. In this data visualization project, I parsed collected information, converted the values to a clean graphic format using D3.js, and finally making it interactive for people to use.

The goal was to start a conversation on public transportation and how it is affected by poverty in Atlanta. This connected the crime rates of neighborhoods with Marta stations and looking at the stop's traffic to see if there is a correlation. I concluded there is indeed a corellation. Presenting this to Atlanta city officials, it gave us a chance to display our interest in starting a conversation on improving the stops, especially as the city continues growing.
